A little bit about me

I’m a life-long learner who’s quick to develop an interest in anything I’m exposed to. Software Engineering allows me to explore my various passions from a technical standpoint. I have experience in building applications with various technologies, most commonly utilizing JavaScript, Python, React, Redux, Flask, and SQL.

Before becoming a programmer, I’ve worked as a personal trainer, a teacher, a physiologist, and an international health education volunteer. In my free time, I enjoy reading: fantasy, adventure, and science fiction some of my favorite genres. I'm especially fond of short stories, as well as anything by authors Neil Gaiman, Haruki Murakami, and Brandon Sanderson.

I love learning about language and culture – I lived in Cambodia for two years and am proficient in basic Cambodian. Getting outdoors is another favorite pastime of mine and I’ve done hiking treks in various countries around the world: Nepal, Indonesia, and Switzerland to name a few.
